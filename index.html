Share Surplus Food, Serve the Hungry

Connecting food donors with NGOs to reduce food waste and feed the needy.

Problem We Solve

Manual coordination between donors and NGOs
No real-time visibility of available surplus
Food goes to waste due to slow response
Lack of a unified donation platform

How It Works

List Surplus Food

Donors list extra food with location & details.

NGOs Notified

Nearby NGOs receive instant alerts on availability.

Accept & Collect

NGOs accept and collect the food from donors.

Platform Managed

Admins monitor activity and manage the system.

Join the Movement

Whether you're a food donor or an NGO, together we can make a difference.
